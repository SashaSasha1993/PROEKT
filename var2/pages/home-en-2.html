Nano is well known in Canada and Europe since its launch in 1999. It's 2016 when the line appeared in the US under the name Nöruk Collection. Both lines, which are the same, except for the name, offer everything you need for any occasion!

Our extensive collection includes first durable and colorful outerwear garments, ready to tackle any challenges Mother Nature has to offer. Our swimwear will keep your kids in the water until it's time to put their beautiful Nanö / Nöruk pyjamas. They are so cute with their funny characters and glow in the dark features! This is all without forgetting an extensive sportswear line, offering styles for both every day wear and special occasions, requiring a touch of chic! And all this in fabric that are always soft and stretchable for extra comfort.

Our designer travel the world to find the best trends for Nanö / Nöruk. We take pride in offering designs children love to play in and «easy to care» to the delight of parents.

Nanö/ Nöruk offer a wide range of items that will fill the needs of kids aged from 0 to 12 years!
