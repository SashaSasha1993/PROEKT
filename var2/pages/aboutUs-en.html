Nano

With autum and winter quickly approaching, all parents are concerned with making sure their children's wardrobe is up to date. First and foremost this means proper outerwear.

We expect our kids to have proper attire that will be both stylish and keep them warm all season long. It should be warm for those windy winter days, waterproof for the rain and slush, but also breathable for sunny days and easy to care for, not to mention reliable! Of course, it should also be convenient for the movement of the baby. Any good jacket should be able to keep you comfortable and warm whenever and wherever you may go, and all this - for a reasonable price!!

Despite the wide variety, picking a high-quality product at a reasonable price is not so simple. In fact, it can often be difficult to distinguish higher quality outerwear from the average counterfeit, which can decieve consumers with a decent appearance "before the first wash," but whose service life is often limited to a single season. To put you at ease, we bring the valued the Canadian NANO trademark to our products which will ensure a worthy place for our outerwear in your baby's closet for years to come. To date, it is difficult to imagine a company that is more renowned for their reliability than NANO. With more than 200 stores worldwide, the Nano brand is synonymous with sleek, reliable, and reasonably priced products, from jackets, trousers, sets, and coats to hats, mittens.

For autumn and spring NANO offers the most optimal variant of seasonal clothing for children from one year to 10 years - a set of jacket and trousers. The set is made of light breathable material and is quite pleasant to the touch. The relatively thin outer fabric of the jacket has a special patented coating of Teflon fabric, developed by DuPont. This coating allows to remove the dirt from your clothes just using a sponge!

The upper fabric remains soft and fine to the touch, but it does not get wet and remains breathable and light. Unlike other manufacturers, NANO uses a patented layer of insulation in its jackets.

POLIFILL - one of the best and most modern materials - is used as a heater. The fine of which POLIFILL fibers have excellent heat-insulating properties and perfectly hold the shape, preventing deformation of the clothes. The amount of filler in the jacket - 90 g - is more than enough to keep you warm even in a chilling frost.

The inner fabric of the jacket is a soft fleece. MICROPOLAR is an anti-pilling (non-rolling), elastic, lightweight, soft knitted material. It has unique properties of thermoregulation. The material is pleasant to the touch, dries quickly, does not lose its properties during use, and has an attractive appearance. And, of course, hypoallergenic.

Pants with a lining of soft knitted material are conveniently cut, thanks to a wide elastic band, they sit well with any child's complexion. The inside fabric of the trousers has polyurethane spraying and they won't get wet even when falling on wet ground, so your child can safely sit on a wet swing!

Moreover, just above the ankle, there is an additional internal valve made of a porous membrane material, which is firmly attached to the shoe using a dense elastic band. This means that moisture will not get in and you can run even on wet snow.

NANO has another very successful design feature that allows you to extend the duration of socks for 2-3 years.
